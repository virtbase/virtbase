First of all, if you're reading this article and are looking to delete your Virtbase account, we're sorry to see you go!

We would appreciate any feedback on how we could improve our platform – feel free to [reach out to us](/en/contact)!

You can delete your Virtbase account by following the steps below:

1. Navigate to your [account settings](https://app.virtbase.com/account/settings) in the customer portal.
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page, where you will find the **Delete Account** option.
3. Click on the **Delete Account** button. You will be prompted to **confirm** your deletion.
4. After confirming, your account will be permanently deleted. Please note that this action is irreversible.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please [contact our support team](/en/contact).

We hope to see you back someday!

## When I delete my account, do you remove all my data?

Yes, if you delete your Virtbase account, all personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.
This includes all active Virtbase servers and data linked to the account.

Your email address will also be removed from our mailing list. You will no longer receive emails from Virtbase.
