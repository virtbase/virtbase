All Virtbase servers are protected by **aurologic GmbH**'s DDoS protection. 
This offers excellent protection against large attacks on the one hand, 
while on the other hand, packet filtering is very accurate and fine-grained. 

All common network services and protocols are supported. 
Online games are also perfectly protected with this solution and remain playable throughout the entire attack.

If you have any further questions about DDoS protection, please [contact our support team](/en/contact).

## Do I need to activate DDoS protection?

No, DDoS protection is enabled by default on all Virtbase servers. 
You don't need to do anything else to benefit from the protection.

## Which protocols are protected?

All common network services and protocols are protected. 
These include TCP and UDP-based services. Game server protocols are also recognized and specifically protected.

## Will my server be accessible during an attack?

Yes, DDoS protection is specially optimized for web and game servers. 
The use of challenge response mechanisms ensures that your server remains accessible even during an active attack.
