To prevent your server from being shut down and deleted prematurely, we recommend renewing at least **5 days before expiration**.

## Renewal in the customer portal

To renew your server, follow these steps:

1. Go to your **server** in the [customer portal](https://app.virtbase.com/servers).
2. Click on **Plan** in the navigation bar.
3. Now select the desired package. For a regular renewal, the current package is already preselected.
4. Confirm the renewal by clicking on **Renew**. You will then be redirected to the payment page.

After successful payment, your server will be renewed for another 30 days. The invoice will be stored in the customer portal and sent to you by email.

If you have any further questions about renewal or need assistance, please [contact our support team](/en/contact).

## What happens if I don't renew my server?

We will send you an email **5 days** before your server expires with a reminder to renew your server.

At the end of the term, the server will be **suspended** with another notification. This means that it cannot be started, nor can other
relevant actions such as restoring the backup, reinstalling, or making changes to the firewall be performed.

If your server has been suspended, you have **5 more days** to renew it. After that, your server will be permanently deleted with another notification.
Please note that we cannot restore any data in this case.
